\noindent\textcolor{rulered}{\rule{\textwidth}{0.4pt}}

\noindent\textbf{He aha te wh\={a}inga m\={o} t\={e}nei rangahau / What is the aim of the project?}

\smallskip

\noindent Large Language Models (LLMs)---the technology behind tools like
ChatGPT---are increasingly making decisions that affect people's lives. However,
the values these models follow are typically set by private companies rather than
the public. This project aims to develop and test a method for tuning LLMs to
behave in ways that better reflect New Zealanders' values.

\noindent Your participation will help us understand whether the public prefers
the behaviour of LLMs that have been aligned to public values compared to
unaligned models. This research has been approved by the Te Herenga
Waka---Victoria University of Wellington Human Ethics Committee [H\={o}kai
application reference number to be added].

\bigskip

% --- Section 2 ---
\noindent\textcolor{rulered}{\rule{\textwidth}{0.4pt}}

\noindent\textbf{Ka p\={e}hea t\={o} \={a}whina mai / How can you help?}

\smallskip

\noindent You have been invited to participate because you are a member of the
New Zealand public. If you agree to take part, you will complete an anonymous
online survey. The survey will present you with short scenarios describing how
an AI system behaved in a particular situation. For each scenario, you will be
asked to rate how much you agree with the AI's behaviour on a simple scale. You
will also be asked a few questions about your own values. The survey will take
approximately 15 minutes to complete.

\bigskip

% --- Section 3 ---
\noindent\textcolor{rulered}{\rule{\textwidth}{0.4pt}}

\noindent\textbf{Ka ahatia ng\={a} k\={o}rero ka tukuna mai / What will happen to the information you give?}

\smallskip

\noindent This research is anonymous. This means that nobody, including the
researchers, will be aware of your identity. By answering the survey, you are
giving consent for us to use your responses in this research. Your answers will
remain completely anonymous and unidentifiable. Once you submit the survey, it
will be impossible to retract your answer. Please do not include any personal
identifiable information in your responses.

\medskip

\noindent Personal details will be collected separately only for those who wish
to enter the prize draw. All personal details will be received separately from
the survey data and will be held in confidence. This ensures that your answers
to the survey questions will not be linked to your identity.
